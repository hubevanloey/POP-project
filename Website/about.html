About Joshua

From a very young age Joshua paved his way to become a singer/songwriter. “It has always felt very natural to combine words and music and turn them into songs”. Although the singing came later, the passion was already there.


Always looking for new horizons and armed with a powerful, authentic, raw and versatile voice, Joshua started working with a lot of different local musicians who introduced him to the world of freelance and the studio scene.


During that period, he worked with a wide variety of prime musicians from bands such as Soulsister, Arno, The Scabs, De Kreuners, Guido Belcanto, Helmut Lotti, Rob De Nijs, Marco Borsato, Ilse DeLange, Anouk, John Miles, Tina Turner, Roy Orbison, … To name but a few.


Past decade, Joshua has been involved in several other interesting projects, including the animal welfare project A.R.M. for which he organized a few events to support GAIA & Sea Shepherd. This contribution was the genesis to the “Through These Eyes” CD and a heartfelt co-operation with actor Pol Goossen. A few years ago, Joshua also appeared (vocals & lyrics) on the single CD “Days” from DJ Mr. E, that gave him a little taste of the Tomorrowland experience.


At the moment Joshua is working on project “LINK” with singer/songwriter Kirri Valvekens and author Wim Urgel, which will hopefully see the light of day by the end of 2022. Work is also underway on some new solo material, together with producer & “partner in crime” Roel de Ruijter at Studio Pump It Up.

"It’s really simple. If the project feels right and moves me as to such I can pour my heart and soul in it … you can count me in."

On stage, you will always find him in good company. The last years you may have seen him perform together with Alain “mainman” Smits, Bart Buls, David Ronaldo, Jamie Breeze or Kirri Valvekens, with whom he is now collaborating by the name of The Joshua/Kirri Experience, bringing a nostalgic repertoire from the 60’s, 70’s & 80’s.

The passion goes on …
